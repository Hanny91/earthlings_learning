Home-Tutoring Services

Earthlings Learning aims to provide affordable, fun and inspiring tuition in environmental management, sustainability and earth sciences to students of all ages. We aim to foster knowledge and enthusiasm about how the world works either to prepare for future careers or to simply expand your knowledge.

We currently offer IGCSEs in Environmental Management, Geography and Marine Science. We also offer A-level tuition in Environmental Science

We especially work with people for whom traditional schooling isn’t the right fit, for personal, educational or geographical reasons. We focus on exam preparation for children and young adults to allow students outside of traditional education to attain recognised qualifications and access higher levels of learning.

In a time of unprecedented environmental challenges, we believe that increasing understanding of how Earth systems work, how humans influence them and how to improve this is of paramount importance in our daily lives and workplaces in all sectors.
